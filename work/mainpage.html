About Orbitar

The Orbitar Elevator will whisk you from Earth to our Orbitar Space Station comfortably, safely, and quickly, at an affordable cost. Once arriving at the space station, you can enjoy our vast amount of amenities and facilities, ranging from sightseeing and entertainment to academic research.

The Orbital Elevator

The elevator will transport you from Earth to the space station, 45,000 kilometres above the planet, in less than 12 hours of ascend time. With a fleet of 4 elevators, and a capacity of 40 passengers per elevator, we make it easy for you to book a trip.

Cost: $5800 per return trip

Elevator Amenities

  • Lounge with recliners, televisions, wireless internet access, and large windows
  • Snacks and two meals included, with the ability to upgrade or purchase more meals on board
  • Shared sleeping rooms with comfortable beds, with harnesses available so you won't float out of bed when reaching space
  • Pressurized cabins and climate control provide a comfortable atmosphere
  • Spacious washrooms
  • Wheelchair accessible, including lifts to move between each floor

Safety Features

  • Elevator cables are made of diamond nanothreads—one of the strongest materials in existance
  • Elevator cabins are built with reinforced titanium
  • Both have been engineered to survive impacts from foreign objects, such as meteoroids, with multiple failsafe systems
  • Weather-proof, corrosion-proof, and radiation-proof cables and elevator cabins
  • Frequent maintenance ensures the elevator system is always in safe condition
  • Advanced warning systems to warn of any incoming dangers
  • Crew of trained attendants can help with any concerns or medical emergencies on board

Other Technical Information

  • Maximum operational speed of 80 km/s
  • Each cabin has seven spacious floors with ~800 square feet of room
