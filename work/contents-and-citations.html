About Orbitar

Orbitar whisks visitors from Earth to our space station comfortably, safely, and quickly, at an affordable cost. Once arriving at the space station, guests may enjoy our wide amount of amenities and facilities, ranging from entertainment to academic research.

The Orbital Elevator

Citizen Vacation Class Hotel Rooms

Citizen vacation class rooms provide a comfortable way for visitors who are vacationing at the space station to get some rest after a day of exploration. Pressurized cabins provide a comfortable atmosphere. Drift off into sleep in a unique room that is padded to prevent injuries, and to prevent getting woken up due to collisions.

Citizen Scientist Class Hotel Rooms

For visitors to the space station who want to conduct science experiments on their own without being professionally affiliated with a company or research organization, citizen scientist class rooms are a great way to get started. We provide equipment for you to work with to expand your research, with the ability to do complex calculations. When you're ready to get some rest, pull down our luxurious bed and strap yourself in.

Advanced Scientist Class Hotel Rooms

Advanced scientist class rooms are a great way for professional scientists to work on business and sleep in the comfort of a large, private room. We provide a variety of highly specialized equipment and technology that makes it easier to work with your data. It also comes with a big, luxurious bed!

Please note that due to the sensitive nature of the equipment, you must be affiliated with a professional research organization to book this room.

Orbitar Telescope

View the vast cosmos of the universe with our ultra-high definition telescope! Use our user-friendly computer system to adjust the telescope and its settings with precision, or use the optical viewfinder to view the universe live with your eyes. Take extremely high-resolution images as a keepsake or for research!

Visitors and scientists are both allowed to use the telescope.

Astrochemistry Lab

Using the astrochemistry laboratory, scientists may conduct studies and experiments to understand chemical processes outside of Earth. Our lab has a variety of specialized instruments and chemicals to support research. The lab has an artificial gravity generator installed, so that you can safely and easily conducting experiments, similar to how they can be done as if you were on Earth.

Astrochemistry lab users must be affiliated with a professional research organization.

Particle Accelerator

The Orbitar Collider lets scientists conduct experiments involving particle physics, and how it is different while under the influence of microgravity.

Users of the Orbitar Collider must be affiliated with a professional research organization.

Launch Pad

Our launch pad on board the space station allows scientists to launch and dock small space probes, satellites, and manned capsules. Its state of the art technology design allows for multiple launches per day. Bookings may be made while booking the trip, or while at the space station.

To book a time on the launch pad, users must be affiliated with a professional research organization.
