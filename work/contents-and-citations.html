Middle Introduction Section

Orbitar whisks visitors from Earth to our space station comfortably, safely, and quickly, at an affordable cost. Once arriving at the space station, guests may enjoy our wide amount of amenities and facilities, ranging from entertainment to academic research.

Hotels

Our space station offers comfortable hotel accomodations.

Vacation rooms provides a comfortable way for space station visitors to get some rest after a day of exploration. Pressurized cabins provide a comfortable atmosphere. Drift off into sleep in rooms that are are padded to prevent injuries, and to prevent getting woken up due to collisions.

Research lab rooms are a great way for researchers and scientists to work on business in the comfort of a private room. We provide a variety of equipment and technology that makes it easier to work with your data. When you're ready to get some rest, pull down our ultra-luxurious bed and strap yourself in.

Launch Pad

Our launch pad on board the space station, allowing scientists to launch (and dock) small space probes, satellites, and manned capsules. Its state of the art technology design allows for multiple launches per day. Bookings may be made while booking the trip, or while at the space station.
