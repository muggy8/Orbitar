Main Page: Hotels

Our space station offers comfortable hotel accomodations.

Vacation rooms provides a comfortable way for space station visitors to get some rest after a day of exploration. Pressurized cabins provide a comfortable atmosphere. Drift off into sleep in rooms that are are padded to prevent injuries, and to prevent getting woken up due to collisions. Or, if you prefer to sleep without floating around, you may strap yourself into a sleeping bag.

Research lab rooms are a great way for researchers and scientists to work on business in the comfort of a private room. We provide a variety of equipment and technology. When you're ready to get some rest, pull down our luxurious bed and strap yourself in.
