About Orbitar

Orbitar whisks visitors from Earth to our space station comfortably, safely, and quickly, at an affordable cost. Once arriving at the space station, guests may enjoy our wide amount of amenities and facilities, ranging from entertainment to academic research.

Hotels

Our space station offers comfortable hotel accomodations.

Vacation rooms provides a comfortable way for space station visitors to get some rest after a day of exploration. Pressurized cabins provide a comfortable atmosphere. Drift off into sleep in rooms that are are padded to prevent injuries, and to prevent getting woken up due to collisions.

Research lab rooms are a great way for scientists to work on business and sleep in the comfort of a private room. We provide a variety of equipment and technology that makes it easier to work with your data. When you're ready to get some rest, pull down our ultra-luxurious bed and strap yourself in.

Telescope

View the vast cosmos of the universe with our ultra-high definition telescope! Use our user-friendly computer system to adjust the telescope and its settings with precision, or use the optical viewfinder to view the universe live with your eyes. Take extremely high-resolution images as a keepsake or for research!

Visitors and scientists are both allowed to use the telescope.

Launch Pad

Our launch pad on board the space station, allowing scientists to launch (and dock) small space probes, satellites, and manned capsules. Its state of the art technology design allows for multiple launches per day. Bookings may be made while booking the trip, or while at the space station.

Astrochemistry Lab

Using the astrochemistry laboratory, scientists may conduct studies and experiments to understand chemical processes outside of Earth. Our lab has a variety of specialized instruments and chemicals to support research.

The lab has an artificial gravity generator installed, so that you can safely and easily conducting experiments, similar to how they can be done as if you were on Earth.

Particle Accelerator

The particle accelerator lets scientists conduct research experiments under the influence of microgravity.
